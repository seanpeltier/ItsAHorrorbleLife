Ghosts In The Burbs

By: Sean Peltier

Last Halloween, I stumbled across Ghost In The Burbs. Being a Connecticut native, I was thrilled to find a spooky podcast that's located in Wellesley, Massachusetts. Narrator and star of the podcast, Liz Sower talks about the residents of her small town. What started as a simple post on a bulletin board asking other locals to tell her their own spooky ghost stories, sends Liz down a rabbit hole of ghosts, monsters and much more!

I cannot recommend this podcast enough! Ms. Sower has a way of narrating a story that makes you feel like you're in the sleepy town of Wellesley.

If you enjoy the podcast, be sure to check out Liz Sower's two books Claire (Ghost In The Burbs Book 1) and Lilith: A Ghost In The Burbs Story
