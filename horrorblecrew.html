My name is Sean, and I am a horror addict. There are two things that I'm very passionate about. Web development and the Horror genre. I wanted a horror website that would be fun but also informative. One that touches on all aspects of horror. From the movies and music to the collectibles and conventions. So I decided to ask some of my friends to join me in this venture and here we are.

This site has been a long time coming. It's been a good two years since I came up with the idea for IAHL. I feel like this creation has been sitting in my chest like a xenomorph about to burst through Kane's chest in Ridley Scott's Masterpiece, Alien.

I made sure to recruit others that are just as passionate about the genre as myself. I asked them all to write a little about themselves and to answer some questions so you can get to know them better. I appreciate you taking the time to stop by and hope you will join us as we show you what it's like to lead a Horrorble Life!
