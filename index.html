Welcome to It's A Horrorble Life!. My Name is Sean. I am a web developer, vinyl record collector and most imporatntly, a horror fanatic! The idea for this website has been something I've wanted to do for a long time. I wanted a fun, judgement free zone where the die hard fan and casual watcher can come togther and discuss all things horror. A site where you can discover movies, music, interviews and so much more!

I was lucky enough to have some very good friends who have agreed to help me bring content to this site.
